hermit crabs live in shells. Some hermit crabs are large and others are small. They look like crabs. They smell bad. Their shells come in different colors and patterns.

Hermit crabs live for not very long. People keep hermit crabs as pets. Hermit crabs like to eat dark leafy foods like kale. If you ever own a hermit crab they usually take small bites of food, mostly at night. Hermit crabs have 1 small claw and one large claw on their body like crabs.
